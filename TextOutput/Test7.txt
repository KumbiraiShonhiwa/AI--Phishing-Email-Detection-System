Internship Opportunity – Faculty of Education | Apply by Sunday, 18 May 2025

Dear Kumbi,

The Faculty of Education at the University of Pretoria invites applications for a graduate internship within the Office of the Dean. This internship forms part of UP’s Ready for Work initiative, helping our graduates gain valuable workplace experience.

Do you know who should apply?
Graduates with a relevant UP Bachelor’s or honours degree in a related field, who:

Have an average academic mark of 60% or above
Are South African citizens, aged between 18 and 35
Are not studying full-time (classes after hours preferred)
Are proficient in English (spoken and written)
Internship responsibilities include:

Administrative support (scheduling, minutes, document preparation)
Project coordination and tracking of Faculty initiatives
Communication and event coordination support
Records management and reporting assistance
Support for student engagement initiatives
Important: You don't need any prior work experience. Interns will receive mentorship and training.

Remuneration: R6,500 per month (40 hours/week)

How to Apply
Email the following separate documents (do not combine into one file) to Internship@up.ac.za
Subject line: DF-INT25_EDU

5 Ready for Work certificates
CV
Academic record
Certified proof of degree completion
Cover letter
Certified ID copy (not older than 3 months)
Deadline: Sunday, 18 May 2025
No late applications will be accepted.

Need help with your application?

Please book a consultation on TuksCareers, and one of our consultants will gladly assist you.
